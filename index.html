Frequently Asked Power Washing Questions

The frequency of house pressure washing depends on several factors such as location, weather conditions, and the presence of trees or foliage around your property. Generally, it’s recommended to have your house pressure washed once a year to maintain its cleanliness and prevent the buildup of dirt, mold, and mildew.

While pressure washing is an effective cleaning method, it’s important to use the appropriate pressure and techniques for different surfaces. Our experienced professionals are trained to adjust the pressure according to the surface being cleaned, ensuring safety and preventing any damage. We also use eco-friendly cleaning solutions to protect your property and the environment.

The duration of a pressure washing service varies depending on the size of the area being cleaned and the extent of the dirt and grime buildup. Generally, a typical residential pressure washing session can take anywhere from 1 to 4 hours, while larger commercial projects may require more time. Rest assured, we work efficiently without compromising on quality to minimize any inconvenience to you.

You are not required to be present during the pressure washing service, although you are welcome to be if you wish. Many of our clients prefer to schedule the service when they are away to minimize disruptions. However, we do ask that any gates or areas needing access are unlocked and that pets are secured or kept indoors for their safety and the safety of our technicians. Our team will ensure thorough communication and follow-up after the service to guarantee your satisfaction.
